Welcome

A Legacy of Goan Hospitality

Nestled on the golden sands of Calangute Beach, Hotel Golden Eye has been welcoming guests since 1983. Founded by Michael Mascarenhas and lovingly run by the Mascarenhas family, our boutique hotel offers an authentic Goan experience unlike any other.

Each room tells its own story through vibrant hand-painted murals, warm wooden interiors, and private balconies overlooking the Arabian Sea. Step outside to our legendary Flying Dolphin beach cafe for fresh seafood, cold drinks, and unforgettable sunsets.
