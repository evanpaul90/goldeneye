Flying Dolphin

Beach Side Cafe, Bar & Restaurant since 1983

The Restaurant

A Feast by the Sea

The Flying Dolphin is more than a restaurant — it's an institution on Calangute Beach. For over four decades, we've been serving the freshest seafood, authentic Goan curries, and flavours from around the world.

Whether you're enjoying a lazy breakfast watching the fishermen at work, sipping cocktails as the sun dips into the Arabian Sea, or savouring a candlelit dinner under the stars — every meal at Flying Dolphin is an experience.

Open daily: 9:00 AM – 11:00 PM

Signature Experiences

Goan Fish Curry

Our legendary spicy fish curry made with the day's freshest catch, coconut milk, and traditional Goan spices. Served with steamed rice.

Seafood Platter

A lavish spread of grilled prawns, calamari, fish tikka, and crab — cooked in your choice of style. The taste of the Arabian Sea on your plate.

Sunset Cocktails

Sip on our expertly crafted cocktails — from classic Goan Feni mixes to tropical mojitos — as the sun sets over Calangute Beach.

The Beach Bar

Drinks on the Sand

Pull up a lounger, order a cold beer or a cocktail, and let the waves be your soundtrack. Our beach bar is open all day — the perfect spot to while away a Goan afternoon.

Indoor Dining

Pub 'N' Seafood Restaurant

For a more intimate dining experience, step inside our Pub 'N' Seafood Restaurant. With comfortable indoor seating, a full bar, and an extensive multi-cuisine menu, it's the perfect setting for a special dinner or a rainy-day feast.

The restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner, with a complimentary continental breakfast for hotel guests every morning.
