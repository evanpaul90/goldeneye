## History & Ownership

- **Founded by:** Michael Mascarenhas on 5th December 1983
- **Originally started as:** Golden Eye bar and restaurant
- **Expanded by:** Michael & his brother Mathias Mascarenhas
- **Currently managed by:** Brothers Michael & Mathias Mascarenhas, and sister Theresa
- **Inspiration:** Their father Philip Mascarenhas is the inspiration behind the entire operations
- **Family-run business** with a warm, homely atmosphere
- The property has grown from a bar/restaurant into a full hotel with beachside dining
